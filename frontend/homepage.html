About TrackWise

TrackWise is a final year TYBSC Computer Science project focused on productivity tracking and analysis.


Our Mission

To make productivity tracking simple, visual, and effective.

Our Vision

To empower users with insights that turn habits into growth.


Why TrackWise?


In today’s fast-paced academic and professional life, most people:


  • Feel busy but don’t feel productive.
  • Study for long hours without visible improvement.
  • Struggle with consistency and time management.
  • Fail to identify weak subjects or habits.

TrackWise bridges this gap by converting daily effort into actionable insights.
